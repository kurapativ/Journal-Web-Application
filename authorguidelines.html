GUIDELINES TO PREPARE AUTHOR’S RESEARCH/REVIEW PAPER

All the authors are requested to submit their manuscript in the journal prescribed manuscript only. This is followed to maintain uniform format for all the accepted manuscripts throughout the journal issue. Please follow the steps outlined below when submitting your final draft to the editorijieee@gmail.com . These guidelines include complete descriptions of the fonts, spacing, and related information for producing your manuscripts. Please follow them and if you have any questions, direct them to the chief editor, IJIEEE journal. 



1. The paper format should be in double column and normal A4
    size paper.

2. Font Size: throughout the paper maintain Times new Roman

3. Paper Main Title: 18 point bold centered and leave 6 point space 
  after paper main title.

  Author’s name: 11 point bold centered

  Author’s Affiliations: 10 point bold centered

  Author’s email id: 8 bold centred and leave 10 point space 
         after email ID.

  Main headings in the text: size 12 point capitals bold centered,
  do not  use colon  here.

  Leave 4 point space after the heading.

  Sub Headings: 11 point bold left aligned 
  
 Sub Sub Headings: 10 point bold left aligned. The text 
       continued along with  the subsub heading Sub sub Heading 
	numbering must be start with ‘i’.
