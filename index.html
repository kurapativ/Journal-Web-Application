Welcome to IJIEEE

International Journal of Instrumentation Electronics and Electrical Engineering is an open access international journal peer reviewed online journal that publishes articles in the field of Electronics, Instrumentation, Electrical, Control systems, Automation, Network Security, Embedded Systems, Parallel Processing, Communication, VLSI, Computers in automation, Image processing, Neural Networks, Wireless Sensor Networks, etc.

IJIEEE Journal aims at the publication and dissemination of original peer- reviewed research articles, reviews and discussions on the latest developments in all fields of engineering science and technology. The journal publishes original papers in English which contribute to the understanding of engineering science and improvement of the engineering technology and education. Papers may be theoretical (including computational), experimental or both. The contribution should be unpublished before and not under consideration for publication elsewhere. Many of our journals now use an online submission and peer review system as this can bring a number of advantages to authors, referees, the editorial office and the publishing process in general. The papers for publication in IJIEEE are selected through rigorous peer reviews to ensure originality, timeliness, relevance, and readability. IJIEEE also seeks clearly written survey and review articles from experts in the field to promote insightful understanding of the state-of-the-art and the technology trends.

Frequency: 6 issues per year
